# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

This project welcomes constructive participation. Please be respectful, specific, and focused on the work.

Examples of positive behavior:

- Giving actionable feedback.
- Accepting different experience levels.
- Keeping discussions technical and relevant.
- Assuming good intent while still asking for clarity.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insults, discriminatory language, personal attacks, and publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that do not meet these standards. Repeated or severe violations may result in being blocked from participating in the project.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately through the repository owner profile. Maintainers will review reports and respond as appropriate for the situation.
